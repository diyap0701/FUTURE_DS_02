# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

> **Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Internship (Task 2)**

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the most important factors for the success of subscription-based businesses. This project analyzes customer churn data to identify patterns, understand factors influencing customer attrition, and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and prepare customer churn data.
- Analyze overall customer churn.
- Identify customer segments with the highest churn rates.
- Examine the impact of contract type, internet service, payment method, monthly charges, and customer tenure on churn.
- Generate business insights and retention strategies.

## 📈 Key Findings

### 📉 Customer Churn Distribution
- Most customers remain with the company.
- A significant percentage of customers have churned, highlighting the importance of retention strategies.

### 📑 Contract Type Analysis
- Month-to-month customers exhibit the highest churn rate.
- Customers with one-year and two-year contracts show significantly better retention.

### 🌐 Internet Service Analysis
- Fiber Optic customers have the highest churn rate.
- DSL customers demonstrate better retention.
- Customers without internet service have the lowest churn.

### 💳 Payment Method Analysis
- Electronic Check users are more likely to churn.
- Customers using automatic payment methods show higher retention.

### 💰 Monthly Charges Analysis
- Customers paying higher monthly charges are more likely to churn.

### ⏳ Customer Tenure Analysis
- Customers with shorter tenure have a significantly higher churn rate.
- Long-term customers demonstrate strong loyalty.

### 👴 Senior Citizen Analysis
- Senior citizens have a higher churn rate compared to non-senior customers.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to switch from month-to-month contracts to annual plans through discounts or loyalty rewards.
- Improve the Fiber Optic customer experience by addressing service quality and support concerns.
- Promote automatic payment methods to reduce churn associated with Electronic Check payments.
- Develop targeted retention campaigns for senior citizens and newly acquired customers.
- Review pricing strategies for customers with high monthly charges to improve satisfaction and retention.
